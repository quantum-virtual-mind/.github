# Quantum Virtual Mind

Welcome to **Quantum Virtual Mind**.

We are building at the intersection of **brain-computer interfaces, quantum computing, and personalized AI**. Our work explores how cognitive data, advanced computation, and intelligent systems can come together to create **Quantum Virtual Minds (QVMs)**—digital representations designed to support more adaptive, personalized, and consciousness-driven technologies.

## What we do

- Explore Quantum Virtual Mind systems built from Brain-Computer Interface (BCI) data
- Develop approaches that combine classical algorithms, quantum methods, and multi-agent AI
- Investigate cognitive modeling frameworks such as the **Quantum Connectome**, **Agentome**, and **Mental State Tensor**
- Support applications in mental health, education, personalized AI, research, and immersive media
- Share projects openly so others can learn from, build on, and contribute to them

## Why this organization exists

Quantum Virtual Mind is a home for projects focused on modeling, simulating, and extending human cognition through computation. We aim to create a space for thoughtful experimentation across neuroscience-inspired systems, quantum technologies, AI personalization, and developer-facing platforms that help make these ideas practical and reproducible.
